Privacy Policy

Last Modified on 27 Aug 2025

This Privacy Statement ("Privacy Policy") describes FORTRUST's policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use the FORTRUST's website. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information we collect by other means (including offline) or from other sources. By accessing or using the website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect and Use

FORTRUST uses information we collect to operate, maintain and provide you the features and functionality of the website, to analyze how the website is used, diagnose website or technical problems, maintain security, personalize content, remember information to help you efficiently access your account, monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors, traffic, and demographic patterns.

Information You Provide

You provide us information about yourself, such as your name and e-mail address, if you register for an account with the website. Your name, email address and other information you choose to provide on the website will be forwarded to the University, College or their representatives when you submit an application for University or College admissions via our website.

We may use your email address to send you website-related notices. We may also use your contact information to send you marketing messages. If you don’t want to receive these messages, you can opt out by following the instructions in the message. If you correspond with us by email, we may retain the content of your email messages, your email address and our responses.

Content

You also provide us information in Content you post to the website. Your Content (such as reviews, photos and videos) and metadata about your Content will be viewed by other users. FORTRUST can, but has no obligation, to monitor your Content you post on the website. We can remove any information you post for any reason or no reason.

Cookies

When you use the website, we may send one or more “cookies” – a small data file – to your computer to uniquely identify your browser and let FORTRUST help you log in faster and enhance your navigation through the site. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the website may not function properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.

Log Files & Analytics

When you use the website, our servers automatically record certain information sent by your web browser (log files). FORTRUST also uses third party analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to help understand use of the website. These tools collect information sent by your browser, including cookies and your IP address, and their use of it is governed by their own privacy policies.

How We Share Your Information

We will forward your Personal Information as part of the application form to the University or College or their official representatives when you make the application for admission on the website.

  • Service Providers: FORTRUST may share your personally identifiable information with third parties for the purpose of providing the website to you. Their use of your information will be bound by this Privacy Policy.
  • Business Transfers: As we develop our business, we may buy or sell assets. Customer information is generally one of the transferred business assets in these types of transactions.
  • Compliance with Laws: FORTRUST may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or subpoena or if we believe that it is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request.

How We Protect Your Information

The security of your information is important to us. In the event that personal information is compromised as a result of a breach of security, FORTRUST will promptly notify those persons whose personal information has been compromised, in accordance with the notification procedures set forth in this Privacy Policy, or as otherwise required by applicable law.

Your Choices About Your Information

You may, of course, decline to submit personally identifiable information through the website, in which case FORTRUST may not be able to provide certain services to you. You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging in to your account.

Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. Our website is not directed to persons under 13. FORTRUST does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at info@fortrust.com.

Links to Other Web Sites

We are not responsible for the practices employed by websites linked to or from our website, nor the information or content contained therein. When you use a link to go from our website to another website, our Privacy Policy is no longer in effect.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@fortrust.com.
