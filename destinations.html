Why Study in Australia?

Australia is a leading global education powerhouse with some of the world's best facilities and educators, providing local and international students with a range of quality study options. It's a multicultural, safe, and welcoming society where students can learn and travel in their free time.

Top Cities

  • Sydney: Famous for its harbourfront Opera House and vibrant culture.
  • Melbourne: Known for its arts scene, great coffee, and laneway culture.
  • Brisbane: A sunny, subtropical city with a thriving economy.

Entry Requirements

General requirements include proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL), academic transcripts, and a valid student visa.

  • Undergraduate: Completion of Australian Year 12 or equivalent.
  • Postgraduate: A recognized bachelor's degree.
  • English Language: Typically an IELTS score of 6.0-6.5 for undergraduate and 6.5-7.0 for postgraduate.

Living & Tuition Costs

Costs can vary significantly based on your institution and lifestyle.

  • Average Tuition Fees: A$20,000 - A$45,000 per year for Bachelor's Degree.
  • Estimated Living Costs: A$21,041 per year (as per Australian Government).

Work Rights for Students

International students can work up to 40 hours per fortnight during semesters and full-time during scheduled breaks.

Scholarships

Many scholarships are available for international students, offered by universities and the Australian Government. We can help you find and apply for them.
